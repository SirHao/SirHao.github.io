The International Workshop on Interactive, Mobile and Wireless Sensing Technology (IMWST)

In conjunction with ICA3PP 2023, Tianjin, China on 20-22 October 2023

Call for Papers

Wireless signals have achieved great success in data communication, but they are now being actively used for sensing purposes. Wireless sensing has shown the potential to perceive human targets and the environment around us. However, challenges still exist in enabling sensing and communication to coexist without interference, and effectively improving sensing capabilities using artificial intelligence technology. We invite researchers from a wide range of fields to submit papers to the Wireless and Mobile Sensing Technology Workshop. This workshop aims to share the latest research results and achieve the goal of ubiquitous and intelligent sensing. We welcome original research papers that propose new ideas, discuss new directions, raise open questions, or provoke challenging discussions. Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • Wireless sensing technology and systems (e.g., WiFi, RFID, LoRa, UWB, FMCW radar, light and sound)
  • Sensing with artificial intelligence
  • Coexistence of sensing and communication
  • Novel sensing approaches and techniques
  • Sensor networks and their applications
  • Data processing and analytics for sensing applications
  • Security and privacy issues in wireless sensing
  • Smart cities and IoT applications
  • Environmental sensing and monitoring
  • Wearable and implantable sensing devices
  • Human-computer interaction in sensing systems
  • Machine learning and deep learning for sensing data analysis
  • Energy harvesting and power management in sensing systems
  • Localization and tracking in wireless sensing
  • 5G and beyond for wireless sensing applications

Accepted papers will be presented in the workshop and included in the conference proceedings. We look forward to your submissions and to seeing you at the Wireless and Mobile Sensing Technology Workshop. Submitted manuscripts must be prepared according to IEEE Computer Society Proceedings Format (double column, 10pt font, letter paper) and submitted in PDF file format. The manuscript should be no longer than 6 pages and at least 4 pages. Submitted manuscripts must not contain previously published material or be under consideration for publication in another conference or journal.

Submission link: https://easychair.org/my/conference?conf=ica3pp2023
REMEMBER TO choose the track “ Wireless and Mobile Sensing Technology Workshop (WMST)” to submit papers. Accepted papers will be included in the conference proceedings and published in the IEEE Xplore digital library (indexed by EI). At least one author of any accepted paper must register and present the paper at the conference. After registration, all of the accepted papers will be recommended to SCI journal SPECIAL ISSUE, such as COMNET, Sensors. We will plan it in the future.
If you have any questions, please contact us at: wanglei@suda.edu.cn or caichao08@gmail.com
